## About

**mgnbar.info** is an interactive overview of what is known about the geometry
of the moduli space $\overline{\mathrm{M}}_{g,n}$ of stable $n$-pointed genus
$g$ curves, and of its interior $\mathrm{M}_{g,n}$. Choose a layer above the
table to switch between invariants — the Kodaira dimension, whether the Chow
ring is tautological, and whether point counts over finite fields are polynomial
in $q$ — and click a cell to read off the value together with its references.

It is maintained by [Ignacio Barros](https://sites.google.com/view/ibarros/home)
and [Pieter Belmans](https://pbelmans.ncag.info). The source code and the
underlying data are on [GitHub](https://github.com/pbelmans/mgnbar), where
suggestions and corrections are very welcome.
